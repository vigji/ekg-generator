Patient Monitor Simulator

Real-time ECG, SpO2, and capnography waveform simulator for clinical education.

Open Controller Open Monitor

How to use:

1. Click Open Controller to adjust rhythms and vitals.

2. Click Open Monitor to display the patient monitor.

3. Drag the monitor window to a projector or second screen and go fullscreen (click the monitor).

Note: Both windows must be open on the same device and browser. Changes on the controller appear instantly on the monitor.
