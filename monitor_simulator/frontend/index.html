Patient Monitor Simulator

Free real-time ECG, SpO2, capnography, and invasive arterial pressure waveform simulator for clinical education, ACLS/PALS training, and medical simulation.

Open Controller Open Monitor

How to use:

1. Click Open Controller to adjust rhythms and vitals.

2. Click Open Monitor to display the patient monitor.

3. Drag the monitor window to a projector or second screen and go fullscreen (click the monitor).

Note: Both windows must be open on the same device and browser. Changes on the controller appear instantly on the monitor.

Features

  • 30+ ECG rhythms: Sinus, AFib, A-Flutter, SVT, VT, VF, Asystole, STEMI, WPW, TCA toxicity, Hyperkalemia, and more
  • Real-time SpO2 plethysmography waveform
  • Capnography (ETCO2) waveform
  • Invasive arterial pressure (ART line) waveform
  • Defibrillator with charge and discharge sounds
  • Cardiac pacing with adjustable rate and current
  • SYNC cardioversion mode with R-wave markers
  • Adjustable heart rate, blood pressure, SpO2, and ETCO2
  • No installation needed — runs entirely in the browser
  • Free and open source
